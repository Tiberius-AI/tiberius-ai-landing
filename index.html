Questions, answered

Know exactly how Alfred works.

Is Alfred just another chatbot?

No. Chat is how you direct Alfred, but the value is operational. With your permission, Alfred can prepare and complete connected actions such as lead follow-up, booking, document delivery, status tracking, deposit requests, and invoice reminders.

Does Alfred need my approval before taking action?

You decide. Require approval for every action, allow specific routine actions automatically, or use a mix. Sensitive steps can always remain behind an approval gate.

Does Alfred control my prices or discounts?

No. You provide the price rules, approved offers, discounts, and contract terms. Alfred works inside those boundaries and asks when something falls outside them.

Do I need a DocuSign account?

You need an active DocuSign account to send agreements through the DocuSign workflow. Alfred coordinates preparation, delivery, tracking, and next steps through your connected account.

What are Build Credits?

Build Credits are the usage system for advanced Legend projects such as landing pages, ad creative, research, and custom workflows. Your monthly allowance is included, and Alfred shows expected credit use before paid work starts.

Can I get surprise usage charges?

No. Alfred presents additional usage or Build Credit needs for approval. Nothing beyond your included allowance is silently added.

Is a website included with Legend?

Legend includes access to Build Mode for websites and landing pages. Alfred scopes the requested project, shows the expected Build Credit use, and begins only after approval.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. You describe the outcome in normal language. Alfred shapes the idea, presents a plan, builds an approved version, and gives you a private preview for feedback.

Can Alfred work with my existing website?

Yes, in many cases. Alfred can create standalone campaign pages or work with your current site setup when access and compatibility allow. The plan will make any technical requirements clear first.

Can Alfred work with the systems I already use?

That depends on available connections and access. During setup, you identify your current phone, calendar, CRM, document, payment, and accounting tools. Tiberius confirms what can connect and how each connection will be used.

What happens when Alfred is uncertain?

Alfred pauses, explains what is missing, and asks you or a designated person. You define the handoff rules so uncertainty never becomes unwanted improvisation.

Is my company’s data kept separate?

Yes. Your company’s data, settings, conversations, permissions, and connected actions remain separated from other businesses. Access is limited by the controls and roles you establish.
